About Us


We are a group of university students from 3 different countries, Belgium, Greece and Portugal. We committed ourselves to this work with the intention of helping people with MS, since it is still a disease with little evidence of its onset.

For that, it is necessary to somehow find a way to make life easier for people with this disease and for those who want to help them, so we joined this project to develop the bracelet that will make life easier for nurses and allow patients to gain confidence.

From another angle, this project allowed us to improve our English language skills, time management, creating new connections with people from different cultures and giving a generalized baggage for our professional and personal life. Overall and speaking for everyone, the connection to this project was enriching.
