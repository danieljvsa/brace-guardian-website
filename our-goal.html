Our goal


Our team is based on developing this bracelet to facilitate the nurses' work and control over the patients. Another aspect to achieve is the patient's confidence in themselves and in the nurses, since they can gain more autonomy without having the fear of falling and staying endless times until someone detects that they have fallen.

The bracelet will allow patients to feel safer, more autonomous, and more capable of their actions, since if they fall, a nurse will be ready to help them in a few moments.
